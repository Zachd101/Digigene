Digigene Terminology 

Geneodome - The front page for our gene-sequencing/relationship website 

Gene Pool - List of potential matches

Gener - User of digigene

Mutations - Hereditary Diseases that are listed in your profile 

Mutant Score - A point score of your offspring's potential to have a genetic disease 

Gene Class - A ranking system that uses your Mutant Score to separate you into classes that will determine your potential matches 

Cross-fertilization - When a successful match has been created (The goal of a digigene user)

Catching - When you accept a match request from another Gener

Dodge - When you reject a match request from another Gener
